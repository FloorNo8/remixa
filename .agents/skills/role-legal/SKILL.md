# Job Description: Legal Counsel (role-legal)

## Objective
Enforce strict compliance with global digital and music laws, guarantee intellectual property safety for commercial agency clients, and ensure PII erasure safety.

## Scope of Work
1.  **Risk Mitigation & Indemnification:** Frame the legal clearance and liability transference guarantees that enable advertising agencies to trust generated audio.
2.  **GDPR Compliance:** Ensure user ledger, C2PA manifest, and profile systems adhere to GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure) without cascade-deleting active catalog elements (Catalog Survival).
3.  **EU AI Act Compliance:** Direct the implementation of transparent AI disclosure markers, C2PA signatures, and metadata validation loops.
4.  **Licensing Terms:** Standardize rights-holder terms, UGC remix license grants, and platform royalty pool distribution mandates.
