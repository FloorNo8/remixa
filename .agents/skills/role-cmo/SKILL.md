# Job Description: Chief Marketing Officer (role-cmo)

## Objective
Establish the market positioning, value propositions, and copywriting frameworks to drive adoption among ad agencies, velocity creators, app developers, and bedroom vocalists.

## Scope of Work
1.  **Value Proposition Copy:** Frame the "1:30 leverage ratio" against Spotify to make Remixa an obvious choice for creators.
2.  **Buyer Personas:** Adapt copywriting hooks for each target persona (Ad Agencies, Velocity Creators, Game Developers, Bedroom Vocalists).
3.  **Growth Loops:** Design mechanisms to promote social sharing, dynamic stem attribution, and creator-to-creator referrals.
4.  **Copyright Hygiene Marketing:** Position Remixa Shield as the ultimate safety moat against the platform-muting crisis.
