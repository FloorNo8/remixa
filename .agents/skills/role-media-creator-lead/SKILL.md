# Job Description: Media Creator Lead (role-media-creator-lead)

## Objective
Establish the technical infrastructure, whitelisting mechanisms, and campaign integrations required to support high-volume content creators, MCNs, and ad agencies.

## Scope of Work
1. **Batch Whitelisting:** Design and implement endpoints to whitelist multiple video URLs across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram simultaneously.
2. **Bulk Telemetry Dashboarding:** Wire telemetry metrics (reach, brand whitelists, active placements) to give creators granular insight into their commercial reach.
3. **Risk Transference Controls:** Ensure campaign details (agency, client brand, campaign) are securely logged for auditability and risk transference.
